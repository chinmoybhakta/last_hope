## 📱 Welcome to Last Hope

Last Hope is your personal offline AI survival assistant that works completely without internet connectivity. Powered by advanced language models, it provides expert guidance for wilderness survival, emergency preparedness, and outdoor adventures.

### 🎯 What Makes Last Hope Special

- **100% Offline Operation** - No internet required after initial setup
- **Specialized Survival Expert** - Trained specifically for survival scenarios
- **Multiple AI Models** - Choose from 4 specialized models for different needs
- **Conversation Memory** - Your survival conversations are saved locally
- **Privacy First** - All data stays on your device

## 🚀 Quick Start Guide

### First Time Setup

1. **Launch the App**
   - Open Last Hope from your app drawer
   - Allow necessary permissions (storage for models)

2. **Download Your First Model**
   - Click "Sign In to Download Models" (required for model access)
   - Choose a model from the available options
   - Wait for download to complete (models range from 600MB-900MB)

3. **Start Your First Conversation**
   - Once model is downloaded, click "Start Chat"
   - Select your preferred AI model
   - Begin asking survival questions!

### Model Selection Guide

| Model | Size | Best For | Speed | Expertise |
|-------|------|----------|-------|-----------|
| **TinyLlama 1.1B** | 600MB | Quick answers, basic survival | ⚡ Fast | Good fundamentals |
| **Phi-2 2.7B** | 900MB | Complex scenarios, detailed advice | 🐢 Moderate | Excellent reasoning |
| **Llama 3.2 1B** | 800MB | Instruction following, chat | ⚡ Fast | Better conversations |
| **Qwen 2.5 1.5B** | 900MB | Structured answers, expert guidance | 🐢 Moderate | Best overall |

Different models excel at different tasks:

- Best for: "What's the best way to start a fire?"

#### **For Complex Scenarios**

- Best for: "Can you walk me through building a shelter?"

#### **For Expert Guidance**
- Use **Qwen 2.5** for structured, expert-level advice
- Best for: "What are the medical considerations for long-term survival?"

### Managing Storage

#### **Checking Available Models**
1. Go to Home Screen
2. Look at "Debug Information" section
3. See "Models Found: X" and available space

#### **Deleting Models**
1. Tap **"Manage Models"** button
2. Select the model you want to remove
3. Tap the **delete icon** (🗑️)
4. Confirm deletion

#### **Storage Tips**
- Keep at least 2GB free for new models
- Delete unused models to save space
- Larger models provide better quality but use more storage

### Critical Survival Priorities

#### **The Rule of 3s**
```
You can survive:
• 3 minutes without air
• 3 hours without shelter (extreme conditions)
• 3 days without water
• 3 weeks without food

```
S - Stop: Don't panic, stay put
T - Think: Assess your situation

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### **Model Not Loading**
**Problem**: "Model already loaded" error
**Solution**: 
- Restart the app
- Try selecting a different model
- Use "Manage Models" to refresh

#### **Slow Responses**
**Problem**: AI takes too long to respond
**Solution**:
- Switch to TinyLlama for faster responses
- Close other apps to free memory
- Restart the app if needed

#### **Storage Issues**
**Problem**: "Not enough space" error
**Solution**:
- Delete unused models via "Manage Models"
- Clear old conversations
- Free up device storage

#### **App Crashes**
**Problem**: App closes unexpectedly
**Solution**:
- Restart the app
- Check available storage
- Reinstall if problem persists
